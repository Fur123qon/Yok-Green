About YokGreen!

Discover the story behind our journey, the principles that guide us, and how we're dedicated to empowering our nature

Explore Our Mission

Our mission is restoring forests with transparency.

YokGren was built out of concern for Indonesia’s escalating forest fires and deforestation crisis. We saw awareness spreading online — but real action was still lacking. So we created a platform that transforms concern into measurable impact. By integrating information, volunteering, donations, and tree tracking into one ecosystem, YokGren supports transparent and sustainable forest restoration.

Our mission is restoring forests with transparency.

Explore Our Target

Our target is scalable forest recovery.

We aim to build a transparent system that tracks every tree planted, increases community participation, and supports long-term, sustainable forest recovery. By integrating technology with real-world action, we ensure that every contribution whether through volunteering or donations is measurable, accountable, and aligned with lasting environmental impact.

Our target is scalable forest recovery.
